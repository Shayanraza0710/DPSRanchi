With a perfect collaboration between the students, parents, teachers and management we ensure that education provides ample opportunities for self fulfilment.

Facts About Our School

  • DPS Ranchi is equipped with specialized laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science, allowing students to have hands-on learning experiences and explore practical applications of theoretical knowledge.


  • The school places a strong emphasis on STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education, encouraging students to explore careers in these fields through various projects, workshops, and competitions.


  • Currently, DPS Ranchi is home to over 500 students, reflecting its growth and popularity in the region.


  • DPS Ranchi offers an extensive sports program, with facilities for football, basketball, cricket, badminton, table tennis, and athletics. The school regularly participates in regional and national tournaments, and students often bring back medals.


  • The school is highly committed to environmental sustainability. It promotes eco-friendly practices, such as waste management, rainwater harvesting, and tree plantation drives. DPS Ranchi has a strong eco-club that engages students in various green initiatives.


  • TERRA LAUREATE is an annual event held by the school, showcasing a blend of art, culture, and performing arts. The event serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents in various artistic fields like drama, music, dance, and fine arts.


  • DPS Ranchi has an international reach and regularly collaborates with schools in countries like the USA, UK, and Singapore for academic exchanges, cultural programs, and joint projects, giving students exposure to global perspectives.


  • The school integrates Information and Communication Technology (ICT) into its curriculum, ensuring that students are well-versed in the latest technological tools and resources. Digital classrooms and online learning platforms are actively used for enhanced learning experiences.


  • DPS Ranchi offers a variety of leadership development programs through student councils, peer mentoring, and workshops on communication, problem-solving, and teamwork. These initiatives help students develop leadership qualities and social responsibility.
